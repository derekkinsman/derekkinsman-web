Derek Kinsman

Crafter & Maker of Things

Derek J. Kinsman is a maker of things residing in Guelph, ON, Canada. Sometimes those things require code, sometimes power tools and hand tools, usually it's for other people, and often it's done with others. Derek specialises in radical generalism (thanks for the term Sara Hendren) and he's really good at breaking things for Science!

He is currently looking for new opportunities. Formerly he worked with the mighty fine folks at Siberia, as well as Teehan+Lax on the Labs team.

Most of the projects he has been involved with in recent years were completed under NDA and cannot be shown publicly. If you would like to learn more about Derek's involvement in these projects reach out using your preferred contact details below.
